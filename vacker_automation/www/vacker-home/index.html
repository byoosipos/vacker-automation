About Vacker

Vacker is an outdoor & indoor advertising company based in Uganda & was incorporated in 2014. We pride ourselves in being one of the country's preferred out-of-home Advertising companies specializing in; Digital Signage, Billboards, Street Furniture, Creative & Branding Services.

We specialize in delivering high-impact, visually stunning campaigns that captivate audiences and generate real results for our clients. With our cutting-edge technology, we can bring your brand to life in both outdoor and indoor environments.

We understand that every business is unique, which is why we work closely with our clients to develop customized advertising solutions that are tailored to their specific needs. Whether you're looking to increase brand awareness, drive sales, or promote a new product or service, we've got you covered.
